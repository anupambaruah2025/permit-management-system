## ℹ️ Tips for Online Submissions

1. **Check Operating Hours** - Most agencies 8:30 AM - 4:30 PM, Mon-Fri
2. **Language** - Forms usually in Thai; translations required for foreign documents
3. **Apostille** - All foreign documents need apostille (single certification for all countries)
4. **Consular Legalization** - Some agencies require additional legalization from Thai embassy/consulate
5. **Email Submissions** - Many forms available on websites; check if email submission allowed
6. **Walk-in Required** - Some permits require in-person submission; call first
