CRITICAL CHANGES (2025-2026) - READ THESE FIRST
================================================================================

Three major BOI changes affecting your project:

1. ERC POWER LETTER NOW MANDATORY PREREQUISITE
   - Must obtain BEFORE BOI application (legal requirement)
   - Document: 06_BOI_UPDATES_AND_ANALYSIS.docx, Section 1 UPDATE 2
   - Impact: This is the real bottleneck (2-8 weeks)

2. QUARTERLY E-MONITORING (Effective April 2026)
   - Changed from biannual to quarterly reporting
   - 60-day submission deadlines via e-Monitoring portal
   - 2 consecutive misses = Permanent certificate revocation
   - Document: 06_BOI_UPDATES_AND_ANALYSIS.docx, Section 1 UPDATE 3
   - Impact: Strict compliance required (no grace period)

3. TAX EXEMPTION IS CONDITIONAL ON POST-APPROVAL VERIFICATION
   - CIT doesn't activate at BOI approval
   - Requires 3 verifications: ISO/IEC 27001, Thailand Benefit Plan, PUE (if 8.2.1.1)
   - Document: 06_BOI_UPDATES_AND_ANALYSIS.docx, Section 1 UPDATE 7
   - Impact: 2-6 month delay after construction for tax benefits
